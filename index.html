Epic Survival Adventure!

Monster Survivors throws you into a world overrun by endless waves of monsters! Your mission is simple: SURVIVE as long as possible! Battle through increasingly difficult hordes, collect powerful upgrades, and see how long you can last in this action-packed survival game.

No complicated rules, no lengthy tutorials - just jump in and start blasting monsters right away!

How to Play

  • 1 Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character around
  • 2 Your character attacks automatically when enemies are in range
  • 3 Collect experience gems from defeated monsters to level up
  • 4 Choose new weapons and upgrades when you level up
  • 5 Grab power-ups and health pickups that appear on the battlefield
  • 6 Survive as long as possible and aim for a high score!

Pro Survivor Tips

Keep Moving!

Standing still is a death sentence! Keep moving to avoid getting surrounded by monsters and create space for your attacks.

Build Synergies

Focus on upgrades that complement each other rather than picking random abilities. Create powerful combinations!

Prioritize Threats

Some monsters are more dangerous than others. Learn to identify and eliminate the biggest threats first!

Balance Your Build

Don't ignore defensive upgrades! A mix of attack power, area damage, and survivability is key to lasting longer.
